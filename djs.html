Randy Watson

Well, first off "Randy Watson" is not my real name....this alias comes from one of Eddie Murphy's characters in "Coming to America." All my fellow children of the '80s probably already picked up on that....and if you don't know, check out the movie-- it's a classic! I got my start in 2006 when Dam-Funk gave me the opportunity to be part of his weekly residency (along with Laroj, Billy Goods, Matt Respect & Eddy Funkster). We've got a nice collective here in L.A. called Funkmosphere-- nothin' but top-shelf '80s funk/boogie and modern soul. Definitely check us out if you're not hip to this genre....I guarantee you've never heard anything like it!

Dam-Funk

Damon Garrett Riddick (born June 15, 1971), better known by his stage name Dâm-Funk (stylized as DāM-FunK; pronounced "Dame-Funk"), is an American funk musician, vocalist and producer from Pasadena, California. In 2007, Riddick signed with the L.A.-based record label Stones Throw Records.

Laroj

DJ Laroj is one of LA’s top vinyl selectors and is an original member of Dam-Funk’s renown Funkmosphere crew. He has been a fixture in the LA funk scene spinning at clubs and parties for well over a decade. Recently Laroj was officially recognized by the Los Angeles city council for his service with Funkmosphere – “LAs longest running 80’s funk party.”
